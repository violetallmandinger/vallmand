Mobile Apps Lead — U.S. OEM ICE App Benchmark
Led companion study evaluating ICE vehicle app ecosystems across major OEM brands, translating customer needs into feature prioritization decisions. Revealed a critical engagement gap: 80% of owners use their vehicle app, but only 27% identify as frequent users. "Connectivity and speed continue to be the most significant challenges for ICE vehicle apps. Owners want apps that work seamlessly every time, with core features that are easy to access and perform reliably."
